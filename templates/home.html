About This App

Nutri Compare helps users make informed choices by placing two product nutrition labels side-by-side for quick comparison.

  • Compare nutritional values side by side
  • Check carbohydrates, proteins, fats, sugars, and salt levels
  • View ingredients and highlight differences
  • Make informed and healthier buying decisions

Target Audience

  • Health-conscious individuals aged 18-50
  • People with specific diet requirements
  • Families wanting quick product comparisons
  • Anyone looking to simplify their grocery choices

Definitions

Nutri-Score: This Nutri-Score rates the overall nutritional quality of food products from A (healthiest) to E (least healthy) considering various nutrients such as calories, fat, sugars and salt.

Environmental (Eco) Score: The Eco-Score evaluates the environmental impact of food products from A (best environmental impact) to E (worst environmental impact) considering factors like productino method, packaging, transportation distance and land use.

NOVA Group: The NOVA classification catergorises food products into 4 groups - Group 1 (unprocessed/minimally processed foods), Group 2 (processed culinary ingredients), Group 3 (processed foods), and Group 4 (ultra processed food and drink products) meaning the lower the NOVA rating the better.
